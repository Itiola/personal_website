Public Speaker · Thought Leader · Consultant

I am Babatunde Fortune Itiola, fondly called BFI, a passionate thinker and speaker at the intersection of technology, economics, and data. My work is grounded in the belief that understanding complex systems—whether economic, technological, or social—can empower individuals and organizations to make smarter, more informed decisions.

As a consultant and emerging thought leader, I engage audiences around the practical application of data-driven insights, the transformative power of technology, and the economic principles that shape our world. I strive to make complex ideas accessible and actionable, bridging the gap between theory and practice.

I am currently building a consulting company dedicated to helping organizations harness the power of data, innovate strategically, and unlock new economic opportunities. My approach blends rigorous analysis with strategic thinking, enabling leaders to navigate uncertainty, anticipate trends, and make decisions that drive measurable impact.

Through public speaking, writing, and advisory work, I aim to inspire others to think critically, embrace innovation, and understand the broader forces shaping business and society. I believe that knowledge is most valuable when shared, and that insights are only as powerful as the action they inspire.

When I’m not speaking or advising, I’m exploring emerging trends in technology and economics, seeking new ways to create meaningful impact, and engaging with communities that are shaping the future of business and innovation.
